1/ About land tour

  • - Children from 12 years old and above: Apply the same price as an adult.
  • - Children from 6 – under 12 years old: 75% of adult tour price (no extra bed); charged 100% as an adult tour price if an extra bed is required. Every 02 (two) adults can accompany 01 (one) child from 6 – under 12 years old; for the second, please pay the same price as an adult.
  • - Children under 6 years old: 50% of an adult tour price (no extra bed). Every 02 (two) adults can accompany 01 (one) child under 6 years old; for the second, please pay 75% of adult tour price.

2/ About transportation & other cases

  • - Tickets for airline, train and fast ferry shall be applied as the own policy for each particular transportation company.
  • - At some attractions, children who are over-height compared to the attraction’s rules will be extra-charged.
